BPC-157

Repair & Regeneration

What It Is

BPC-157 (Body Protection Compound 157) is a synthetic pentadecapeptide derived from a naturally occurring protein found in human gastric juice. It is widely studied for its remarkable tissue-repairing properties, particularly in the gastrointestinal tract, tendons, ligaments, and soft tissues. Unlike growth factors, BPC-157 acts through multiple signaling pathways to promote angiogenesis, modulate inflammation, and accelerate cellular repair.

Mechanism of Action

BPC-157 exerts its effects through upregulation of growth hormone receptors, increased VEGF expression, and modulation of the nitric oxide (NO) system. It promotes angiogenesis (formation of new blood vessels), regulates inflammatory cytokine cascades, and protects gastrointestinal mucosa. Its cytoprotective effects extend to the liver, heart, and central nervous system in preclinical models.

Research Summary

  • GI Healing: Numerous animal studies demonstrate accelerated healing of gastric and duodenal ulcers, colitis, and intestinal anastomosis — BPC-157 restores gut barrier integrity.
  • Tendon & Ligament: Rat studies show up to 2x faster Achilles tendon healing. Human case reports suggest efficacy for chronic tendinopathies.
  • Systemic Protection: Hepatoprotective, cardioprotective, and neuroprotective effects documented in preclinical research.

General Use Cases

  • Gastrointestinal disorders (IBS, leaky gut, IBD, ulcer healing)
  • Sports injuries (tendonitis, ligament sprains, muscle tears)
  • Post-surgical recovery and wound healing
  • Joint health and connective tissue repair

Full Dosing Protocol

Exact Dosing Protocol

  • Standard dose: 250-500 mcg taken once or twice daily
  • Loading phase: 500 mcg 2x daily for first 2-4 weeks
  • Maintenance phase: 250-500 mcg daily after initial loading

Injection Frequency

  • Subcutaneous injection into abdomen or affected area
  • Morning and evening administration for optimal plasma levels
  • Oral forms available with reduced bioavailability (~10-20%)

Reconstitution Guide

  • Use sterile bacteriostatic water (0.9% benzyl alcohol)
  • Reconstitute 5mg vial with 2mL water = 2.5mg/mL concentration
  • Refrigerate at 2-8°C, use within 30 days of reconstitution

Cycling Plan

  • Cycle length: 4-12 weeks depending on condition severity
  • Break period: Minimum 4 weeks off between cycles
  • Annual max: 2-3 full cycles recommended

  • Preferred: Lyophilized powder in sealed vials from reputable suppliers
